Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) Analysis

Our laboratory is equipped with cutting-edge ICP instruments for elemental analysis:

  • ICP-OES (PlasmaQuant 9000 Elite) - Ideal for multi-element analysis with high sensitivity.
  • ICP-MS (PlasmaQuant MS Elite) - Ultra-sensitive detection for trace element analysis.

These instruments allow precise and reliable elemental composition determination across various sample types.
